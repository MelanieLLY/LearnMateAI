Project Name: LearnMateAI AI - Collaborative Modular Learning Platform

Short Description: LearnMateAI is an AI-powered study platform that synthesizes instructor materials and student notes to automatically generate summaries, flashcards, and quizzes. Instructors initialize learning modules by uploading core reading materials and defining key learning objectives; students can supplement these with their own study notes. The system utilizes AI to synthesize both sources, automatically generating tailored summaries, flashcards, and practice quizzes. LearnMateAI distinguishes between Instructor-assigned Quizzes and AI-generated Self-practice, providing intelligent feedback on practice results. To assist instructors, the platform generates anonymous aggregated reports on class-wide performance, helping identify common knowledge gaps.

User Personas:
- Alex (Student): Wants to integrate his own notes with the professor's lectures to generate a personalized study set that fits his specific learning style.
- Sarah (Instructor): Wants to ensure students master core curriculum concepts and needs a high-level overview of class-wide challenges to optimize her next lecture's focus.

User Stories:
- As an instructor, I want to upload module-specific materials and specify focus areas so that the AI-generated content remains aligned with my teaching goals.
- As an instructor, I want to view an aggregated summary of class-wide difficulty points (without identifying individual students) so that I can address common misconceptions in class.
- As a student, I want to supplement official readings with my own study notes, ensuring the generated flashcards cover my personal knowledge gaps.
As a student, I want to engage in a "heuristic feedback loop" where AI hints help me master the material during my study sessions.
